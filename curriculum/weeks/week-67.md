### Day 1 — Peer review fundamentals (60 min)

**Objective:** Study how to write constructive academic peer reviews

#### Timed schedule
| Block | Minutes | Activity |
|-------|---------|----------|
| Read | 20 | [NeurIPS reviewer guidelines](https://neurips.cc/Conferences/2022/ReviewerGuidelines) |
| Read | 15 | [How to review a paper](https://www.cs.columbia.edu/~hgs/etc/reviewing-guide.html) |
| Lab | 25 | Hands-on in `notes/week-67//` |
| Deliver | 10 | Write deliverable + commit |

#### Source documentation (open these — no external hunting)
- [NeurIPS reviewer guidelines](https://neurips.cc/Conferences/2022/ReviewerGuidelines) (~20 min)
- [How to review a paper](https://www.cs.columbia.edu/~hgs/etc/reviewing-guide.html) (~15 min)

#### Lab steps
1. Write notes/week-67/peer-review-guide.md summarizing review structure
1. List 5 questions every KT/GraphRAG paper review should ask

**Deliverable:** Peer review guide notes
